# App Store review notes for Poker Edge

Paste this into App Store Connect > App Review Information > Notes.

---

Poker Edge is a tracking and study utility for live poker players. It is NOT a
gambling app and contains NO real-money or simulated-money gambling, no wagering,
no purchase of chips, and no games of chance. Nothing of monetary value is won
or lost in the app.

WHAT THE APP DOES
- Lets a player record their own live poker sessions (start time, buy-in, result)
  and track their bankroll. This data is stored on-device.
- Shows a directory of public poker rooms with their published rake and comp
  structure, and a transparent, explainable "rake-friendliness" score.
- Shows a public schedule of live poker tournaments (for example WSOP and WPT)
  with reminders and calendar integration.
- Lets a player keep private notes on opponents they observe in public card rooms.
- Provides study material away from the table: preflop range charts, a strategy
  guide, a quiz, and an equity calculator.

GUIDELINE 5.3 AND TABLE-ASSISTANCE COMPLIANCE
The study trainer is explicitly study-only. It is designed for pre-session and
post-session review, never for real-time use during a live hand. A persistent
on-screen banner in the Study area states: "Study mode. Do not use at the table.
Live electronic assistance violates house rules and can forfeit your action."
The app does not provide live in-hand advice and does not connect to any live
game.

AGE GATE
On first launch the user must confirm they are 21 or older. The attestation is
stored. The listing is rated 17+.

RESPONSIBLE GAMING
A 1-800-GAMBLER link is present in the Profile tab and in any welfare prompt.

PRIVACY
- No account is required to use the app. Core data is stored locally on-device.
- Opponent notes are the user's own private observations of people in public card
  rooms. The app uses descriptive labels by default, does not request or import
  other people's personal information, and contains no facial recognition.
- The app reads public reference data (rooms, schedules) from our own backend API.
  The app itself performs no web scraping.

IN-APP PURCHASE
Poker Edge Pro is an auto-renewing subscription (monthly and annual) that unlocks
advanced analytics and study features. It is sold through Apple In-App Purchase.

TEST ACCOUNT
No login is required. All features are reachable without an account. To review
Pro-only features without purchasing, the reviewer may use a sandbox subscription,
or we can enable a review entitlement on request.
