## Description

The serious live player's companion. Poker Edge gives you data, not vibes.

TRACK EVERY SESSION, OFFLINE
Start a timer the moment you sit down. Log buy-ins, rebuys, tips, and your
result. Everything works with no signal, because card rooms are dead zones.
Your data syncs to your own device storage and is yours to export any time.

KNOW YOUR REAL NUMBERS
See your true hourly rate and win rate by room, stakes, game, day, and time of
day, all computed from your own results. Find your most profitable room and your
most profitable game. Track variance and downswings honestly.

FIND THE RIGHT ROOM
A directory of poker rooms with their rake structure, comps, and a transparent
rake-friendliness score you can tap to see exactly how it is built. Lower rake
cap, no promo drop, deeper buy-ins, and better comps all show up clearly.

NEVER MISS A TOURNAMENT
The full live tournament schedule including WSOP and WPT. Filter by week, month,
city, or series. Set a reminder before any event and add it to your calendar.

READ YOUR OPPONENTS
Tag a player with a description and a type, then log the hands they show down.
Build a read that follows you across sessions and rooms.

STUDY AWAY FROM THE TABLE
Preflop range charts, a player-type strategy guide, a quiz trainer, and an
equity calculator. This is a study tool. Do not use electronic help at the
table. It violates house rules and can forfeit your action.

PLAY YOUR BEST OVER LONG SESSIONS
Break reminders, a session stop-loss and win goal, and an honest fatigue signal
built from your own results help you avoid tilt and avoid playing tired.

Poker Edge is a tracking and study tool. No real-money gambling happens in the
app. You must be 21 or older. If gambling stops being fun, call 1-800-GAMBLER.

FREE AND PRO
Free: session and bankroll tracking, basic schedule, room directory.
Pro: full analytics, your personal most-profitable room and game, unlimited
player notes, the full range library, the quiz trainer, schedule alerts, and
data export. Pro is an auto-renewing subscription billed through your Apple ID.
